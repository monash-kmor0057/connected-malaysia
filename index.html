Connected Malaysia

The growth, usage, and user experience of telecommunications infrastructure in Malaysia

01. Rapid Infrastructure Growth

Over the past decades, Malaysia's telecommunications infrastructure has undergone a dramatic evolution. This chart illustrates the transition from traditional fixed telephone lines (DEL) to the explosive adoption of mobile cellular and broadband subscriptions.

02. The Normalization of Digital Usage

As infrastructure improved, the way Malaysians consume data and interact with the internet shifted significantly. This section explores the current trends in digital consumption and daily communication habits.

03. The Reality of Quality of Experience (QoE)

Beyond raw infrastructure numbers, the MCMC Quality of Experience data reveals the actual satisfaction of users. Here, we examine the perceived stability, reliability, and challenges faced by everyday consumers.

04. Regional Disparities in Network Speeds (2020 - 2026)

Utilizing Ookla's open datasets, we map the evolution of network speeds across Malaysia. This geographical visualization highlights the ongoing digital divide between urban centers and rural areas.

Data Sources: Data.gov.my (MCMC Telecommunications Statistics), MCMC BQoES Report 2024, Ookla Open Data
