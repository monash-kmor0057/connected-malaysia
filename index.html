Major Events Driving Broadband Adoption in Malaysia

  1. 2008 2010

    Fixed

    HSBB

    Government and Telekom Malaysia rolled out nationwide fibre broadband.

  2. 2010

    Fixed

    National Broadband Initiative

    National goal: fibre in cities, wireless broadband in rural areas.

  3. 2013

    Mobile

    4G LTE start

    Malaysia's first commercial LTE networks launched nationwide.

  4. 2015

    Fixed

    HSBB2 & SUBB

    Phase two extended high-speed fibre to suburbs and towns.

  5. 2020

    Mobile / Fixed

    JENDELA

    Expanded 4G coverage, fibre access, and 5G readiness nationwide.

  6. 2021

    Mobile

    5G start

    Commercial fifth-generation mobile services began rolling out.
