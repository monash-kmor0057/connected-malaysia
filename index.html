Major Events Driving Broadband Adoption in Malaysia

  1. 2008 2010

    Fixed

    HSBB

    Government and Telekom Malaysia rolled out nationwide fibre broadband.

  2. 2010

    Fixed

    National Broadband Initiative

    National goal: fibre in cities, wireless broadband in rural areas.

  3. 2013

    Mobile

    4G LTE start

    Malaysia's first commercial LTE networks launched nationwide.

  4. 2015

    Fixed

    HSBB2 & SUBB

    Phase two extended high-speed fibre to suburbs and towns.

  5. 2020

    Mobile / Fixed

    JENDELA

    Expanded 4G coverage, fibre access, and 5G readiness nationwide.

  6. 2021

    Mobile

    5G start

    Commercial fifth-generation mobile services began rolling out.

SECTION 3 | Digital Experience in Malaysia (BQoES 2024)

Data come from MCMC’s Broadband Quality of Experience Study (BQoES), 2024 wave, for fixed (FBB) and mobile (MBB) subscribers. The Customer Satisfaction Index (CSI) is a 1–5 score (higher is better) for streaming, browsing, gaming, and voice or video calls—each activity is scored on its own, not as one overall number. Other charts cover importance–performance priorities, satisfaction gaps, top platforms, dissatisfaction reasons, and who participated.
