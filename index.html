Connected Malaysia

How broadband transformed Malaysian digital life

Malaysia has become far more connected over the last two decades. Faster infrastructure has enabled new digital lifestyles, and overall experience has improved — but gaps remain across regions and activities.

Key Takeaways

📶

Malaysia's connectivity has grown dramatically over the past two decades.

💬

Internet is essential in daily life, led by communication, entertainment and transactions.

⭐

Experience quality has improved, but gaming and some services need more attention.

🗺️

Speed and experience improvements are uneven across regions.

⚡

Better speed does not automatically mean equal digital experience.
